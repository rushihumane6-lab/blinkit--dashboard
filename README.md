# Blinkit Sales & Outlet Performance Dashboard
An interactive business intelligence dashboard that analyzes Blinkit sales performance across outlet locations, sizes, types, product categories, and customer ratings.

# Purpose
This dashboard helps stakeholders monitor overall sales, compare outlet performance, identify high-performing product categories, and evaluate customer satisfaction metrics. It supports data-driven decisions for inventory planning, outlet expansion, and sales optimization.

# Tech Stack
Power BI
  - Data visualization and dashboard development.
SQL
  - Data extraction, transformation, and querying.
Excel / CSV
 - Source data storage and preprocessing.
DAX
  - Calculated measures and KPI metrics.
   
# Data Source
Blinkit retail sales dataset
Includes transaction-level information such as product category, sales amount, outlet location, outlet size, outlet type, customer ratings, and item visibility metrics.

# Feature Highlights
KPI Summary Cards
 - Displays Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, and Average Rating at a glance.
Interactive Filters
 - Allows filtering by Outlet Location Type, Outlet Size, and Outlet Type for focused analysis.
Outlet Establishment Trend
 - Visualizes sales trends over the years to identify growth patterns and peak periods.
Fat Content Analysis
 - Compares sales contribution from Low Fat and Regular products.
Item Type Performance
 - Ranks product categories by total sales to highlight top-performing items.
Outlet Size & Location Comparison
 - Shows how sales vary across different outlet sizes and geographic tiers.
Outlet Type Performance Table
 - Provides a detailed comparison of sales, average sales, item count, ratings, and visibility across outlet types.
Business Value
- The dashboard enables management teams to quickly identify high-revenue outlets, optimize product assortment, monitor customer satisfaction, and make strategic
  expansion decisions based on location and outlet performance.

# Key Points
-Analyzed $1.20M Total Sales across multiple outlet types and locations.
-Evaluated 8,523 products/items sold through Blinkit outlets.
-Tracked Average Sales (141) and Average Rating (3.9) to measure business performance.
-Compared sales by Outlet Size (Small, Medium, High) and Location Tier (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3).
-Identified top-performing Item Categories and product segments.
-Analyzed the impact of Fat Content (Low Fat vs Regular) on sales.
-Examined sales trends based on Outlet Establishment Year.
-Built interactive filters for dynamic business exploration.

#Business Impact & Insights
Revenue Insights
  -Total revenue generated reached $1.20M.
  -Supermarket Type1 contributed the highest sales among outlet types.
  -Tier 3 locations generated the highest sales, indicating strong demand in developing markets.
Product Performance
  -Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods emerged as the highest-selling categories.
  -Low-fat and regular products both contributed significantly to overall sales, with regular products leading slightly.
Outlet Performance
  -Medium-sized outlets generated the highest sales contribution.
  -Older outlets (established around 2018) showed peak revenue performance, suggesting mature outlets drive stronger sales.
Customer Experience
  -Average customer rating of 3.9/5 indicates generally positive customer satisfaction.
  -Item visibility metrics helped identify products requiring better shelf placement and promotion.
Strategic Recommendations
  -Increase inventory for top-selling categories.
  -Focus expansion strategies on Tier 3 and high-performing outlet formats.
  -Improve visibility and promotions for low-performing product categories.
  -Monitor customer ratings to enhance customer retention and satisfaction.
